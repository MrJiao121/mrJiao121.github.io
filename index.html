About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of passionate university students committed to preserving the beauty and health of our marine environments. Our campaign focuses on educating the community about the harmful effects of conventional sunscreens on coral reefs and promoting the use of reef-safe sunscreens.

The Issue

Why Reef-Safe Sunscreen Matters

  • Coral Reefs at Risk: Chemicals like oxybenzone and octinoxate found in many sunscreens contribute to coral bleaching, damaging delicate marine ecosystems.
  • The Impact on Marine Life: These substances can affect the growth, reproduction, and overall health of marine organisms.
  • Global Consequences: Coral reefs support a quarter of all marine species; their decline affects biodiversity and fishing communities worldwide.

Our Mission

Educate. Inspire. Change.

Our goal is to:
  • Raise Awareness: Inform the public about the negative impacts of certain sunscreen ingredients on marine life.
  • Promote Alternatives: Encourage the use of reef-safe sunscreens that protect both your skin and the ocean.
  • Engage the Community: Foster a community of environmentally conscious individuals committed to making sustainable choices.

How You Can Help

Switch to Reef-Safe Sunscreen

  • Look for sunscreens labeled "reef-safe" or "oxybenzone-free."
  • Choose mineral-based sunscreens with non-nano zinc oxide or titanium dioxide.
